---------------VOCABULARY------------------

Stock Data (NIFTY 50) :
Top 50 companies in India's stock market

RSI (Relative Strength Index):
Tells if a stock is oversold(<30 = possible buy)

DMA (Daily Moving Average):
Average price over a number of days 
(eg: 20-DMA = avg of past 20 days)

A crossover is when one line crosses above another 
(eg: 20-DMA > 50-DMA)

Backtest :
Test your strategy on pas data to see if it works
